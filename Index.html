What's my Akan Name?

Ghanian Akan Name Generator

This web application allows you to input your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born,then depending on your gender outputs your Ghanian Akan Name

Ghanian Akan Name History

"Akan" names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'dayname' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
